Popular anime genres:

  • Adventure

    Typically, the adventure genre mainly focuses on undertaking epic quests and travelling around the world. Adventure anime covers a wide range of shows and is also flexible enough to cover multiple episodes.

    Popular adventure anime shows:
    • Fairy Tail
    • Hunter X Hunter
    • One piece
  • Action

    A high level of rigorous action mainly charecterizes the action anime genres. A considerable part of the action genres comprises fluid animation, battle scenes, and super engaging elements that activate your adrenaline system.

    Here are some famous examples of action anime.
    • Full Alchemist
    • Naruto
    • Trigun
  • Comedy

    The central thme of a typical comedy genre is to make the viewer laugh. A comedy anime that doesn't make its watchers at least giggle , let alone laugh,has failed its purpose.

    Here are popular comedy anime shows.
    • Gintama
    • Nichijou
    • Daily lives of high school boys
